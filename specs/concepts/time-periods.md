## Time Periods
Tasks are categorised by their position relative to the present:

| Period       | Range             |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Long Overdue | > 2 weeks past    |
| Overdue      | 0 – 2 weeks past  |
| Present      | This week         |
| Soon Due     | 1 – 3 weeks ahead |
| Long Due     | > 3 weeks ahead   |
